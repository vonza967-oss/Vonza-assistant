FAQ

The obvious questions, answered cleanly.

Do clients have to fully customize the assistant before paying?

No. The flow is sign in, unlock the workspace, then customize the assistant inside the paid experience.

What happens after payment succeeds?

The client returns to the Vonza workspace and lands in the post-purchase setup flow with Overview, Customize, and Analytics.

Can the client add Vonza to the website themselves?

Yes. The workspace includes install guidance and the embed handoff needed to put the assistant on the site.

Does Vonza invent business information?

No. It is designed around the client’s website and lets them refresh the assistant’s website knowledge from the workspace.
