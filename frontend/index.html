FAQ

The important questions, answered directly.

What does Vonza do beyond website chat?

Vonza starts with the website front desk, but the paid workspace adds Home, Customers, Front Desk, and Analytics so the owner can operate from one place. Inbox, Calendar, and Automations are optional connected tools for customers who want that deeper workflow.

Does Vonza support Gmail and Google Calendar?

Yes, as optional connected tools when Google is connected and enabled on the deployment. Vonza can sync Gmail and Google Calendar for review, drafts, scheduling context, and approval-first actions. If Google is not connected, the core front-desk workflow still works safely.

Does Vonza send replies or automations autonomously?

No. Vonza is approval-first. It can draft replies, follow-ups, campaigns, and some calendar actions, but the owner still reviews and approves sends or changes.

How does Analytics track results?

Vonza tracks count-and-proof results it can conservatively tie back to real routing, follow-up, campaign, booking, quote, checkout, or complaint-recovery paths. It does not claim full revenue attribution unless the proof path exists.

What setup is required?

At minimum, add your website, tune the front desk, import knowledge, and install the snippet. Then run a real test conversation and confirm the lead, contact, and outcome path. Connect Google later if you want the optional connected tools.
